# Employee Awareness Guide

## What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers impersonate trusted organizations to steal sensitive information.

---

# Common Targets

- Employees
- Banking customers
- Students
- Corporate users

---

# How Employees Can Stay Safe

- Verify sender email addresses
- Avoid clicking unknown links
- Never share passwords through email
- Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Report suspicious emails immediately

---

# Importance of Awareness

Cybersecurity awareness significantly reduces the risk of phishing attacks within organizations.
