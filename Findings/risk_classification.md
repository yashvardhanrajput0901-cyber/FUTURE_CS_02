# Risk Classification

| Email Sample | Classification | Risk Level |
|---|---|---|
| Fake PayPal Verification | Phishing | High |
| HR Salary Update | Suspicious | Medium |
| GitHub Notification | Safe | Low |
| Banking Security Alert | Phishing | High |

---

# Risk Levels

## High Risk
Emails attempting credential theft or malware delivery.

## Medium Risk
Suspicious emails requiring further investigation.

## Low Risk
Legitimate emails with no malicious indicators.

---

# Conclusion

Proper classification helps organizations prioritize responses and reduce phishing risks.
