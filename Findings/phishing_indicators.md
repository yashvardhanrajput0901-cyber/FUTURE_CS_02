# Phishing Indicators Analysis

## Common Indicators Identified

### 1. Spoofed Sender Address
Attackers used domains similar to legitimate organizations.

Example:
support@paypa1-security.com

---

### 2. Urgent Language
Emails created panic using messages like:
- "Your account will be suspended"
- "Immediate action required"

---

### 3. Suspicious Links
Embedded links redirected users to fake login pages.

---

### 4. Grammar and Spelling Errors
Several phishing emails contained poor grammar and formatting issues.

---

### 5. Malicious Attachments
Some emails included suspicious ZIP or executable attachments.

---

### 6. Credential Harvesting
Fake login pages attempted to steal usernames and passwords.

---

# Conclusion

The analyzed phishing emails used social engineering, spoofing, and malicious URLs to deceive users and steal sensitive information.
